## Tech Stack Review FoodRescue Sync

Your proposed tech stack is modern, robust, and highly practical for an SDP-II (Software Development Project) MVP. Here is a quick breakdown of why it works and one minor detail to keep in mind

 
Flutter & Riverpod Excellent choice. Flutter is the industry standard for cross-platform development (building for iOS and Android simultaneously), saving you double the development time. Riverpod is a modern, compile-safe state management solution that avoids the nesting headaches of older alternatives.


 
Firebase Highly efficient for rapid prototyping. It eliminates the need to build and maintain a custom Node.jsPython backend from scratch.


 💡 A minor technical note You listed Firebase as a Local Database. Firebase is actually a Cloud Backend-as-a-Service (BaaS). While it does support offline data persistence, if you need a dedicated, lightweight on-device local database to pair with it, look into Isar or Hive for Flutter.
 
 


 
TensorFlow Lite & Google Maps API Perfect fits. TF Lite handles on-device machine learning efficiently (e.g., predicting food expiration patterns), and Google Maps is essential for your Surplus Radar real-time tracking.
