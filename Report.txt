📋 **HOW TO PREPARE OUR PROJECT REPORT**
Here is how we need to translate our proposal into the final report structure.

### 1️⃣ Front Page

* **How to prepare:** Use the uni template.
* **Title:** FoodRescue Sync
* **1-Line Description:** *A real-time, rapid-response circular logistics platform connecting commercial food donors with NGOs and consumers to eliminate food waste.*

### 2️⃣ Problem Statement & Objectives

* **How to prepare:** Copy from our proposal, but condense it.
* **Core Issues:** Commercial food waste, landfill emissions, and urban food insecurity.
* **3 Objectives:** Build the circular supply chain, create revenue recovery for businesses, and combat poverty-driven food insecurity.

### 3️⃣ Finalized Features (By User Persona)

* **Donors:** Inventory Management, Expiry Tracking, Flash Sales, Donation Logging.
* **NGOs:** Surplus Radar, Bulk Claiming.
* **Consumers:** Discount Marketplace, Custom Alerts.

### 4️⃣ ER (Entity-Relationship) Diagram

* **How to prepare:** Draw a diagram showing how these Firebase collections connect:
* **User Entity:** UserID, Role (Donor/NGO/Consumer), Location, Contact Info.
* **FoodItem Entity:** ItemID, DonorID, Name, ExpiryDate, Status (Available/Claimed/Sold).
* **TransactionClaim Entity:** TransactionID, ItemID, BuyerClaimerID, Timestamp, Type (Donation vs. Sale).

### 5️⃣ Tentative UI Pages (Mobile Screens)

* **Auth Screen:** Sign up/Login + role selection.
* **Donor Dashboard:** Scan items, track expiry, list surplus.
* **Surplus Radar (Map):** Real-time local map pinpointing available food for NGOs/Consumers.
* **Discount Marketplace:** Consumers browse/buy near-expiry meals cheap.
* **Donation Ledger:** Donors view automated tax logs & download receipts.

### 6️⃣ Tentative APIs (Firebase Triggers/Rest Format)

* `POST /auth/register` – Registers a new user with a specific role.
* `POST /inventory/add` – Donors upload food via manual input or barcode.
* `GET /food/surplus-nearby` – Fetches food listings based on GPS coordinates.
* `PUT /food/claim/{id}` – Reserves bulk food for an NGO / updates status.
* `GET /donations/receipts` – Generates tax-deductible logs for donor businesses.

### 7️⃣ App Review (Competitor Analysis)

*Each of us needs to test one app and drop findings into a simple chart:*

* **Too Good To Go:** Great for consumer surplus bags, but lacks direct NGO donations or B2B bulk features.
* **Olio:** Great for peer-to-peer sharing, but relies too much on manual listings and lacks smart expiry tracking.
* **Food Rescue US:** Great for volunteer delivery routing, but lacks a consumer marketplace or business revenue recovery.
